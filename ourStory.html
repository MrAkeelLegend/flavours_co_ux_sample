My Story as a Baker

An engineer by profession, but always desired to be a culinery artist from childhood, my passion to baking and culinary experiments initially sparked through my grandma & mom. They are the guru’s of my home a baking journey. I started making home delicious for my family and neighbeours around us when i was child. but after my birthday cake for a one year old cutie, rocked the party in my neighbourhood back in 2018, it’s when i decided why not I spread the wings and start my own Food Boutique instead!!


Food Boutique by Uthpala

After the years of culinery journey with my Grandma & mom, Finally I’ve decided to start my own food boutique. That’s what FlavoursCo is...! An home based baking culinery boutique, started initially in instagram and now steadily growing as a trademark of quality, delicious, and full of love among our clients. We love Baking, so preparing your favourite cakes ansd delicacies never make us bored. Simply contact us and get done your favourite delicacies, prepared with full of love from our whole family!
